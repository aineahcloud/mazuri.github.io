Green Planet Agritech:Revolutionizing Urban Farming in Nairobi Green Planet Agritech is at the forefront of sustainable agriculture in Nairobi, Kenya. Specializing in indoor hydroponic farming, they produce high-quality greens that cater to both local and export markets. Their innovative approach reduces water usage and space requirements, making farming more efficient and environmentally friendly. By focusing on sustainability, Green Planet Agritech aims to address food security issues and promote healthier eating habits within urban communities.
